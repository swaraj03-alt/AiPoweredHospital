Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, appointments, and AI-powered healthcare solutions. We’re here to make your experience smooth, transparent, and stress-free.

You can book an appointment online via our website or mobile app, choosing your preferred doctor and time slot.

Yes, all patient data is securely encrypted and stored, ensuring complete privacy and confidentiality.
